About Gabi Fries

Anywhere you go in the Philippines, you’ll find communities that value locally grown crops one of the most iconic among them is gabi (Colocasia esculenta). Inspired by this humble yet versatile root crop, Gabi Fries was created as a fresh and innovative way to celebrate a traditional Filipino ingredient. What started as a simple idea to transform gabi into a flavorful and modern snack has now become an appealing entry point for young entrepreneurs and food lovers. Through continuous improvement in preparation, texture, and flavoring, Gabi Fries evolved into a unique product with strong potential in the food industry. Its use of a local, affordable, and sustainable ingredient highlights the value of Filipino agriculture while introducing a fresh alternative to common snack offerings
